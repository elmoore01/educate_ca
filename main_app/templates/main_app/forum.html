We Look Forward to Having You Join Us Virtually

February 18, 2021 | 10:00am - 2:00pm

The mission of the Educate California Funders Forum is to provide our collaborative partners and other California non-profit organizations with access to grant-makers that focus on empowering communities and youth. Since 2003, we've introduced nearly 60 corporations and foundations to more than 5,000 non-profit directors that have attended our Funders Forums.

WHAT IS INCLUDED IN THE 2021 CONFERENCE COMPARED TO AN IN-PERSON YEAR?
