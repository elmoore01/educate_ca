Bridging the Gaps

Educate California's mission is to provide scalable solutions that connect youth, and those who serve youth, with resources to help prepare our future workforce. We offer workshops, forums, and other special events both online and in person. Our signature program is the LifePrep Academy.

The LifePrep Academy: College, Career and Life Planning curriculum connects families with trusted education resources, while teaching students and their parents the knowledge, attitudes and skills needed to enable clear, high quality communication and collaboration with school counselors.
