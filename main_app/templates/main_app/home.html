Bridging the Gaps

Educate California's mission is to provide effective, scalable solutions that connect people with resources and opportunities to accomplish their goals. We offer workshops, forums, and other special events both online and in person. Our key programs include the LifePrep Academy and the Funders Forum.

The LifePrep Academy College and Career Planning curriculum connects families with trusted community resources, while teaching students and their families the knowledge, attitudes and skills students need to enable clear, high quality communication and collaboration with school counselors. To date, LifePrep Academy has helped more than 10,000 students throughout California graduate from high school, college and career ready.

Educate California Funders Forums bring together nonprofit organizations with corporate, foundation and governmental funders. Our Forums feature engaging conversations with grant-makers, along with a moderated question & answer session where funders discuss their individual approval processes, best practices, and advocacy.
