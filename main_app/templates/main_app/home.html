Bridging the Gaps

Educate California's mission is to provide effective, scalable solutions that connect parents with schools, and nonprofit organizations with grant-makers.

Through our LifePrep Academy College and Career Planning curriculum, we teach students and their families the college knowledge, attitudes and skills students need to graduate from high school college and career-ready. LifePrep Academy engages parents and enables clear, high quality communication and collaboration with school counselors.

Since 2003, we've provided nonprofits with access to grant-makers that focus on communities and youth, by introducing nearly 60 corporations and foundations to more than 5,000 nonprofit directors that have attended our Funders Forums. Numerous nonprofits have reported receiving funding and establishing long-term relationships with funders as a result of attending one of our Forums.
