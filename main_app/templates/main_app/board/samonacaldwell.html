Board Member

SAMONA CALDWELL (Board Chair)

Government Affairs Coordinator, State Farm

Samona Caldwell is a Government Affairs Coordinator with State Farm Insurance Companies in California. Samona’s territory extends from the northern part of Los Angeles County to the Mexican Border and includes Nevada.

As a Government Affairs Coordinator, Samona’s primary goal is to assist in building relationships between State Farm associates and Congressional and State legislators to foster a positive business environment. She also works with legislators in her territory to educate them on public policy issues that relate to the insurance industry.

Samona began her career with State Farm in 1995 as a Homeowners Claim Representative before transitioning to Public Affairs in 2006 and ultimately to Government Affairs in 2015.

Mrs. Caldwell serves as a Board Member for the Valley Industry and Commerce Association and as Vice Chair for the Los Angeles African-American Women in Policy Institute (LAAAWPI). She's also an Advisory Board Member for the Los Angeles Brotherhood Crusade and serves as Educate California's Board Chair.

Samona holds State and City wide positions with the Diversity Coalition Committee, Signal Hill, CA and the CA Department of Insurance. She is also a member of the Public Relations Society of America (PRSA) and an alumnae of Leadership CA, 2020.

Samona enjoys working out, reading and hiking with her family. She is married and lives in Signal Hill, CA with her husband, two kids and Terrier mix.
